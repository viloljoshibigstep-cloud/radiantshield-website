How It Works

Five steps. Full traceability.

Every action is logged in an immutable audit trail for HIPAA, GDPR, and DPDP Act compliance.

1

Ingest

C-STORE, REST API, or batch from PACS/RIS

2

Anonymise

Metadata + pixel PHI removal, Alias ID assigned

3

Validate

39-tag PS3.15 check, SHA-256 integrity hash

4

Route

DICOMweb, REST, or C-STORE to target vendor

5

Restore

Re-identify results, deliver back to PACS

All modalities: CT · MRI · Ultrasound · PET · X-Ray · Mammography · CR · DX · NM · SC
Capabilities

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Configurable Anonymisation Profiles

Strict, AI-Friendly, and Research presets. Per-tag Keep/Remove/Replace/Offset actions. PS3.15 codes auto-populated by modality.

Pixel-Level PHI Redaction

OCR and computer vision detect burned-in text across ultrasound, secondary capture, and nuclear medicine. Automated image masking.

Independent Assurance Gate

39-tag PS3.15 validation on every outbound study. Blocks transfer and alerts on any residual PHI. Zero-trust, zero-exception.

Encrypted Secure Ledger

UUID-based alias per study. AES-256-GCM encryption with client-controlled keys. Deterministic restoration, lossless DICOM fidelity.

Multi-Protocol Routing

DICOMweb STOW-RS, REST API, or C-STORE SCU. Declarative rules match studies by modality and body part to the right vendor.

Real-Time Monitoring

Throughput and latency dashboards. Failure alerting with SLA breach detection. Full admin visibility across all sites.
